# SWE Finalize

Use this when work is ready to hand off, record, commit, or close.

## Workflow

1. Explain what changed, why, and the key paths touched.
2. Summarize verification evidence and any manual checks.
3. Reflect on what was learned and what remains risky or deferred.
4. Provide completion guidance: todo evidence, commit/PR note, docs, or release step.
5. State boundaries clearly; do not claim unverified completion.

## Success criteria

- The handoff is short, factual, and evidence-backed.
- Remaining work is explicit rather than hidden.
